Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about car valuation and selling

How accurate is the online car valuation?

Our online valuation tool provides an estimate based on current market trends and the information you provide. For the most accurate valuation, we recommend contacting our team for a physical inspection of your vehicle.

What factors affect my car's value?

Several factors affect your car's value, including make and model, age, mileage, condition, service history, number of previous owners, fuel type, and market demand. Additional features and modifications can also impact the value.

Can I sell my car directly to Autocap Solution?

Yes, we offer car buying services. After valuation, you can choose to sell your car directly to us. We provide competitive prices and handle all the paperwork for a hassle-free selling experience.

What documents do I need to sell my car?

To sell your car, you'll need the original Registration Certificate (RC), insurance policy, PUC certificate, service records, original purchase invoice, and valid ID proof. Having a complete service history can help increase your car's value.

Can I use my current car's value for financing a new car?

Absolutely! We offer exchange programs where you can use your current car's value as a down payment for your new car purchase. This can significantly reduce your loan amount and monthly EMI payments.
